Particles

Particles are a vital part of the Japanese janguage and make the difference between "My dog is eating," and "I am eating my dog,". I'm not quite sure I can give you every meaning of every particle but I can give you the most standard uses of each one. For example, the particle に has maybe 10 or so different uses. I wont be able to give you any example sentences either, but I'l try my best to list each particle's main uses as concisely and easy to understand as possible.
